How To Play

You an a orange rectangle. To control him you use the arrow keys(the buttons you see to control on mobile).

There are 2 types of enemys green and yellow. At the end of the game there is a boss that takes 10 hits to kill.

To kill enemys you need to jump on the enemys heads. To beat a screen you need to kill all enemys on it.

At the end of a level you will see a green wall at the end at the end of the screen. That is how you play the game.
